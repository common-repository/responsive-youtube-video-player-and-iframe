This plugin transform any iframe and oembed into a clean responsive iframe and video player (youtube, vimeo, dailymotion...)

== Description ==

Do you ever dreamed of having a video player that fits the size of the window of your visitor including those who visit your site with their smartphone? Well this wordpress plugin is very simply and without configuration enable you to make fluid the size of video players. This plugin works with iframe codes offered by the platforms of videos online and also with oEmbed managed by WordPress codes!

== Installation ==

1. Upload `wp-yrvp.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
